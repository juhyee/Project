The World is Changing. So Are We.

By: The Coca-Cola Company Nov 20 2017

For years, we’ve given you the drinks you want and love. And as times change, we want to grow and change with you.
That’s why we are listening carefully and doing more. We are putting you at the center of all we do. Your tastes and needs come first.
We call this Our Way Forward.

How we’re changing

Reducing sugar

While sugar in moderation is fine, too much sugar isn’t good for anyone. That’s why we’re rethinking many of our recipes. Coca-Cola Life,
for example, is sweetened with a blend of cane sugar and Stevia leaf extract. It has 35% fewer calories and less sugar than Coca-Cola.
